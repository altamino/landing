SUPPORT

This page is currently being filled in as the project is still in development.

For any question or issue — account problems, bugs, reports, or anything else — the fastest way to get help is to contact our volunteer team directly:
  • Message our volunteer support bot on Telegram: @alttrinity_bot
  • Found a bug or want to report a security issue? You can also open an issue on our GitHub page.
  • Want to chat with the team or other users? Join our Telegram.

Since AltAmino is a small, in-development project, response times may vary — thanks for your patience!
